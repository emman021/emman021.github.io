Managing Enterprise Servers

This course focuses on managing enterprise servers with a DevOps culture. In this course, students learn the essentials of running a DevOps environment in Linux servers, configuring and managing services, and how to monitor and leverage alerting in the DevOps environment by using de facto standard monitoring practices and Ansible as its configuration management; the student will also learn how to collaborate using Git and use Immutable environment using Containerized technologies, and cloud services. This course is also designed to mirror the current real-world scenarios we have and how to solve them. The course aims to provide students with the knowledge of managing enterprise servers in a DevOps environment. It includes using a common repository and configuring and managing using Ansible, with knowledge of key components to monitor the resources, using containerization technology to create an immutable infrastructure, and applying this knowledge in cloud services.

Reflection and Learnings

Making sure that corporate servers are well-maintained and kept up to date is one of the most important components of managing them. This entails periodically applying security patches and upgrades and keeping an eye out for any potential problems or vulnerabilities with the servers. Making sure that corporate servers are correctly configured to fit the demands of the firm is another crucial component of managing them. Making sure that the servers are effectively linked with the organization's network and other systems entails putting up the necessary security safeguards, such as firewalls and access restrictions. Balancing the demands for flexibility and agility with those for security and stability is one of the problems of operating business servers. The IT infrastructure must be flexible enough to accommodate and support new applications and technologies as the business environment evolves continuously. To ensure that the servers can handle the organization's changing needs, considerable planning and cooperation are required. Overall, maintaining enterprise servers is a challenging endeavor that necessitates a thorough comprehension of both the technical facets of server administration and the organizational business requirements. It is crucial for ensuring the IT infrastructure of the company runs smoothly and securely.
